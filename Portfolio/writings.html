When they come at you from every side, you dodge, you fight back, you block, you strike hard.....
You try to hold on.
They fall back then,
You think you are a mountain, a stone, an army of one
But they come back, stronger, faster, darker, and harder to evade.
You bring up your sword and shield, you cut, you hit, you stab, you push them away.
They disappear, and the moment you think they are gone they show up from nowhere,
This time they are full of evil, hate, will do anything just to bring you down.
You come at full power, with anger in your eyes, and a heart that have nothing to lose.
You win your last fight ...or so you thought
Suddenly they show up with massive forces, every soldier is enough to tear you into pieces.
And then they combine to form a stabber...
You think for a moment, every time I tried to fight I won.
They didn't kill me, so they made me stronger.
I won't fall back now, there must be a way to win.
I`ve been put in this place by the creator.
And I know he gave me the power to handle.
Oh god please help me!
And then your fear is gone, your faith is higher, and your power is multiplied.
And ... you win the fight
You take some rest and then...
The old days come back , this time is horrible, darker than night, scarier than a beast, screaming like thunder.
You think again for a moment, every time I tried to fight I won.
But this time I am different.
They didn't kill me but they made weaker.
There is no way to win this unless he helps me again.
But ....
The help hasn't come yet.
You are guilty, you are being punished, you are being purified.
You realize that you can't win, so you simply give up.
Put down your sword and shield, sit on the floor like a dead corpse.
Life has passed, 8 months had passed, everybody forgot about you, and you are the only one with pain.
You are not strong enough to stand up again.
And you are not weak enough to end your life.
Just go back home and hide.
Don't talk about this with any one.
It will be you secret, your internal compassion for yourself.
You are decaying.
You don't know how or when it will end.
But you know that he will save you some day, and all you can do now is...
Go home and hide, and leave it all behind.
